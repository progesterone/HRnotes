## Early Life
* Born in a low income household
	* godfather paid for his education
	* at age 20 he was studying chemistry with Paul Karrer at the university of Zurich
	* his main interest was chemistry within plants and animals
		* earned a doctorate in just four years of research on the chemical structure of animal substance chitin in 1929
## Career
* Originally was going to persue a artistic career
	* however chemistry and it's theoretical knowledge entranced him
* 1929 he worked at Sandoz Laboratories
	* began studying Drimia Maritima, (squill) and ergot
	* researching lysergic acids, he first isolated and synthesized from his findings LSD on 16th of november, 1938
		* originally LSD was to be a respiratory and circulatory stimulant
		* 5 years later, revisiting research, he accidently consumed some and discovered it's psychedelic effects accidentally.
		* Three days later on 19th of april 1943 he intentionally ingested 250 micrograms of LSD
			* this day is now known as Bicycle Day.
* His discovery of LSD influenced several psychiatrists
	* Ronald A sandison
		* developed its use in psychotherapy which gained international acclaim
* Later discovered [[4-acetoxy-DET]]
